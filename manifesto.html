Manifesto

A concise foundation for a post-capitalist upgrade.

  1. Strategic Public Guilds – Energy, finance, data, health, space, biotech & transport remain socially owned.
  2. Worker Dividend Funds – 20 % voting shares in every firm ≥ 100 employees, controlled by worker-elected trustees.
  3. Carbon Budget is Law – Annual cap declines 7 % per five-year plan; Scientific Panel lock-in.
  4. Universal Basic Services – Healthcare, K-16 education, housing assistance, public transport, internet, legal counsel—free at point of use.
  5. Capital Curtain – Large FX exit requires worker + locality + central-bank super-majority consent.
  6. Public Campaign Finance Only – Private donation or dark-money lobbying = felony with 10-year director ban.
  7. Digital Sortition Chamber – 0.5 % of citizens randomly selected each year; 90-day suspensive veto on eco-social laws.
  8. Local Commune Sovereignty – Cities keep 40 % of tax and can veto extractive projects.
  9. Innovation Commons – Publicly funded patents open-source after 3 years or compulsory licence at 2 % royalty.
  10. Land-Value Commons – Gains > CPI + 2 % taxed at 100 %; revenue funds local basic-income top-up.
